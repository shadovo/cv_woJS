Projects

During my time in C3L's App-developer program [2011-2013] I had the opportunity to work on some truly amazing projects, working with everything from strict XHTML 1.1 to bleeding edge node.js and native iOS apps. In late 2012 my 5 month internship at Amazing Applications AB started. Amazing Applications are the creators of the beautiful top selling iOS-app, Green Kitchen. I even got to be a part of their big new release, the iOS-app Healthy Desserts. As a part of the big release I, with the help of the amazing designer Jimmy Poopuu, created two super crisp retina websites for the apps.

In addition to be a part of the huge Green Kitchen success I rewrote Amazing Applications very first app, Wkly. A delightful app for week numbers, translated to 5 different languages now with full iOS 6 and iPhone 5 support. As a part of the release we once again created a website just as beautiful as the app itself.

Green Kitchen

Green Kitchen is an inspiring source of organic and tasty vegetarian food, with ingredients straight from the nature and a bare minimum of gluten, sugar and dairy products used. Enjoy and be delighted by creative recipes.

Healthy Desserts

Healthy Desserts is an inspiring source of organic and tasty desserts for each and every sweet tooth out there. The recipes are filled with natural sweeteners, healthy fats, whole grains and fresh fruit that will tickle your taste buds.

Wkly 2.0

Wkly is a strikingly simple and graceful app that help you with the tedious task to keep track of the week number. Wkly got it all covered at your fingertips!

Future

As of right now I'm once again back in school, this time for Java! I love learning new things and have grown really fonud on server side development. My first look at server programming was with node.js in late 2011. Since then I've learnt Python and gotten started with Ruby on Rails in my spare time.

My current goal is to be as good as possible on generic server development. In an ever changing world I dont want to be tied down to just one language. I want to work with what is best for that situation. New syntax is something I can pick up really quickly as I go and with a big foundation of server knowledge to bild on I hope to find a workplace where I can grow and learn even more.
